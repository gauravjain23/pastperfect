Past Perfect Heritage Management is an archiving and research agency based out of Mumbai and specializes in institutional and family archiving. We started our journey in 2016 with the intention of spreading the art of archiving so your inherited legacy, your treasured papers, photographs and heirloom items are stored safely, and made useable for future generations. As archivists, we help you in locating/identifying historically significant material that tell your story and develop repositories for the physical preservation of such material. Our expertise is in discovering and documenting the stories, experiences, milestones and accomplishments that fuel the way people connect with their organisations, communities, and each other.
We are a team of professionals with a passion for archiving, research and oral history
